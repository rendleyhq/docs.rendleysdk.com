    Variable add

    add: (rotationSecond: number, rotationFirst: number) => number

    Composes the two D8 operations.

    Taking ^ as reflection:

    E=0 S=2 W=4 N=6 E^=8 S^=10 W^=12 N^=14
    E=0 E S W N E^ S^ W^ N^
    S=2 S W N E S^ W^ N^ E^
    W=4 W N E S W^ N^ E^ S^
    N=6 N E S W N^ E^ S^ W^
    E^=8 E^ N^ W^ S^ E N W S
    S^=10 S^ E^ N^ W^ S E N W
    W^=12 W^ S^ E^ N^ W S E N
    N^=14 N^ W^ S^ E^ N W S E
